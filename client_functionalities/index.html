Client Functionalities

1. View and Follow Assigned Schedules

  • Clients can view their complete weekly schedule, which includes workout and diet templates for each day assigned to them by their mentor.
  • They just have to follow the schedule as mentioned in the template.

2. Save and Submit Workout Reports to Mentor

Flow of the Process

When the client fills out their daily report for each exercise, the following two fields are required:
1. Client’s attained weight
2. Client’s attained reps.

After completing the inputs, the client can save the report in the database and then send it to the mentor for review.

Example of Email Report Received by the Mentor
Subject: Ram’s Monday Workout Report

Hello Mentor,
Please find below the daily workout report submitted by your client Ram for Monday, 10th Nov 2025.

Exercise Name Sets Suggested Weight Reps Range Client’s Attained Weight Client’s Attained Reps
Bench Press 3 sets 20kg / 25kg / 30kg 10 / 8 / 6 20kg / 26kg / 28kg 10 / 7 / 6
Squats 4 sets 40kg / 45kg / 50kg 12 / 10 / 8 38kg / 45kg / 52kg 12 / 10 / 7
Shoulder Press 3 sets 15kg / 20kg / 25kg 10 / 10 / 8 16kg / 18kg / 25kg 9 / 10 / 8

Note: The same report is saved in the client’s workout database.

3. View Previous Workout Details

  • Clients can access all past reports date-wise.
  • They can view the exact exercises, sets, weights, and reps for strength exercises completed on any past day.
  • This helps them review their workout history and track their consistency over time.

4. Edit Profile

  • Update personal details: name, age, height, weight.
  • where Age and weight are essential details for the trainer to consider when creating or assigning personalized workout plans.
