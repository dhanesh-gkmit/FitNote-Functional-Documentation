Business Use Cases

Simple, practical ways the gym uses FitLog every day.

1) Client Management

  • Trainers create client accounts and manage all clients in one place.

2) Workout Plan Delivery

  • No more WhatsApp or paper.
  • Trainers assign daily workout/cardio/diet plans digitally.

3) Daily Check-ins

  • Users finish workouts and submit a quick report.
  • Trainer gets instant updates by email/notification.

4) Progress Tracking

  • Charts and history show volume, PRs, and improvements.
  • Helps trainers make better decisions quickly.

5) Communication Reduction

  • Fewer manual messages.
  • The app handles reminders and reports automatically.

6) Time Saving for Trainers

  • One template can be reused for many clients.
  • Saves hours every week.

7) Paperless Management

  • All plans, reports, and progress are stored in the app.
  • No notebooks or files to manage.

8) Better Client Experience → Higher Retention

  • Clients can see their progress clearly.
  • They stay motivated and continue training longer.
